Softwear

Through different works of “knitted software art” the “Softwear” exhibition sheds light on the shared nature and historical connection between software and textiles. By blurring the boundaries between what is soft and what is hard, and by challenging the gendered associations of both code and textile, the artworks build on feminist theories and encounters with technology, which contribute to demystifying computation and computer science in general, making the field more approachable to those who have been historically pushed away.

Browser Chance Music
- imperceptible internet activity revealed -

Browser Chance Music (BCM) creates a space for a radically different relationship between user and software. Activating this audiovisual system with your own smartphone gives a sonic and visual presence to the evanescent and imperceptible internet activity that emanates from an object which is often quite personal, but largely unknown. Defamiliarizing the smartphone and revealing the aesthetics of its operation opens the doors to a new relationship with our constant companion.

Interactive BCM

On Friday October 15, 2021, you are invited to participate in a live installation of BCM at the Royal College of Music (KMH). This is a drop-in installation. You can join any time between 15h and 18h. To join the BCM performance, you will connect to a specifically configured WiFi network with your favorite mobile device. While on the network, every packet that your applications send and receive from the Web will be passed to a custom built sonification system. Sound in synthesized in realtime depending on who owns the server being communicated with, where the server is located, how much data is being exchanged and how active the connection is are fused into immersive sonic and visual experiences.

Lilla Salen

Browser Chance Music at KMH, in installed in Lilla Salen. From the ceiling of this hall, an audio dome hangs, with some thirty speakers. We use it to create an immersive audio with the applications that you use on your device.
